About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science of Hong Kong Baptist University. I am supervised by Dr. WAN, Renjie. I received my master's and bachelor's degree in Communication Engineering from the School of Information and Communication Engineering at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2022 and 2019. Now, I am also a research intern at NVIDIA.

My research interests include deep learning and its applications on face recognition and video understanding.
